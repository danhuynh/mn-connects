Grant Applications

Requests for funding are accepted year-round. Applicants will receive acknowledgement of the receipt of their application and a board member will contact the organization if more information is required. Applications received after the due date will not be reviewed until the following due date.


Due date for 2002:
May 31st, 2002
Sept 28th, 2002


Grant applications should include the following:

  • A one page summary with budget request - no more than $5,000 (2 copies)

  • A grant proposal - A narrative describing the mission and the operations of your organization as a whole and the specific project for which you are requesting funds

  • Budget review

  • Operating budget

 

*If any of this information is missing, the application will not be considered until that information is made available.
