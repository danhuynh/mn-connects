What We Look For:


We are a program that is connected with the Irwin Andrew Porter Foundation(IAP). With that in mind we have to follow the same guidelines as set by IAP.

Most importantly we look for innovative ideas that foster connections within a community. This means that we want our money to go to projects that are going to snowball and effect a lot of different people. Also we want a bang for our buck. This means we are going to want to get the most out of our money.
The way we will determine this is by budget reviews that is required in the grant request packet. WE WILL ALMOST ALWAYS VISIT YOUR PROJECT AREA. So be prepared to speak with representatives from our grant committee.
