History of Minnesota Connects:

For a year, the Irwin Andrew Porter Foundation contemplated creating a youth group that would extend from their own foundation. They wanted to offer the students an opportunity to experience and explore the fundamentals of creating a foundation. The Minnesota Business Academy caught their interest after a recommendation from BestPrep, a business education organization.
